Founder - President

Kwamiga is the architect behind the Asta Protocol. A serial founder with a history of scaling 0-to-1 ventures, he specializes in turning systemic market chaos into structured intelligence. His mandate is clear: dismantle the opacity of Ghanaian real estate and build the definitive operating system for verified ownership and risk management.

Hubert provides the structural integrity of the Asta Protocol. An enterprise architect with a heavy focus on security (CISSP) and governance (TOGAF), he ensures that Asta is not just an app, but a fortress. He translates complex business requirements into secure, scalable infrastructure, bringing an institutional grade of engineering rigour to the Ghanaian real estate market.

Alexander is the product engine behind the Asta Protocol. A serial innovator with two successful exits in the past six years, he specializes in using technology to compress complex workflows. Previously, as CEO of Apsy, he pioneered AI-driven software to automate mobile app development. Now, he applies that same rigorous logic to Asta, architecting the product strategy that turns market chaos into structured intelligence.

Asta isn’t just a listing site; it’s a protocol for truth. We are a team of engineers, analysts, and scouts obsessed with dismantling the opacity of the market.
